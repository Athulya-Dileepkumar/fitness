Cycling Vs Running:Which is better?

Cycling Vs Running

**Cycling vs Running:** Both are excellent cardio exercises that improve heart health and burn calories. **Cycling** is low-impact and easier on the joints, making it suitable for beginners and people with knee issues, while **running** burns more calories in less time and helps improve bone strength and endurance.
